About Us

Government Primary School Pethgam Wagoora is a community-centered learning institution dedicated to nurturing young minds from Nursery to Grade 5. Established in June 2005, the school has been providing quality primary education under the Samagra Shiksha Scheme.

Our focus is to build a joyful, inclusive and stimulating learning environment where every child feels safe, confident and motivated to explore their full potential.
